FEATURES

Bulk SMS

SMS Striker provides easy accessible, simple and user friendly UI compared to any other Bulk SMS service provider in India and International. Our UI allows one to import numbers in bulk of any format. It also provides a basic copy - paste option.

File SMS Service

This feature permits uploading files in xls, xlsx and txt format with bulk database. We as the Bulk SMS Service Provider has made known to perform SMS campaigning’s in bulk volume in one go and in no time.

Unicode SMS Service

Being the Bulk SMS service provider, we are feasible and flexible to send SMSes in multiple languages using a special feature named Unicode SMS Service that lets SMS to send in regional languages like Telugu, Hindi, Tamil, Gujarati, Malayalam and in international languages like Arabic, Urdu, Chinese and Greek, etc.

Custom SMS Service

Custom SMS Service permits to send personalized SMSes as and when required. For example: SMS can be sent to an individual based on their name, location, occupation and more.

Template

SMS Striker is the leading Bulk SMS service provider in India that enables to add and save SMS templates for sending SMSes multiple times as often as needed to save time.

Missed Call Service

SMS Striker provides Missed Call service along with Bulk SMS Service, which is often offered additionally based on enterprises choice. Missed Call Service is useful to know choices, to gather feedbacks / voting of their customers.

Groups & Contacts

This feature helps in creating multiple groups & contacts easily which will enable to send SMS promptly to the targeted customers.

Long Code Service

Long Code service is an innovative service used to convey text based information to your prospective clients and gives an esteemed experience of using unlimited keywords with extracts of reports and analytics. It is a 10 digit number and are extremely a good option when you want to reach international clients.

Dedicated Long Code

Dedicated Long Code number is explicitly assigned to one particular organization which cannot be given to any other company having the feature of unlimited keywords.

Shared Long Code

Shared Long Code can be allotted to multiple organizations limited with assigned keywords. Keywords provided to one particular organization will not be shared to any other company.

Unlimited Keywords

Based on the business need, unlimited keywords are permissible to one particular company that are not disclosed to any other company.

SMS Reply

SMS Reply is sent as an acknowledgement for any of the services availed that are opt Shared, Dedicated or Unlimited Keyword.

Web Short URL

This feature converts a regular or Long Web URL into its shortened form to come up with an abbreviated version. The user can generate several number of Short URLs for one Web URL which portraits the access of the targeted audience. Such Short URLs helps keeping a track of reports and analytics.

Image Short URL

In general, every Image needs web presence for generating Short URL. Whereas SMS Striker facilitates to create Image Short URL dynamically without copying into browser.

Video Short URL

This feature allows you to upload video related to your product and service and generates Short URL instantly, to promote / share that video with the targeted customers through a SMS.
Promoting a Video for any instance through any marketing media is expensive. Hence, this option helps to promote videos out of our service with minimal effort unlike any other media.

Audio Short URL

Now-a-days, audio reach through OBD (Out Bound Dialer) is expensive and consumes lot of time. Propagating Audio in Short URL is a simplified technology.

Bulk SMS Service Provider

Bulk SMS Service is one of the fastest and easiest ways for the Enterprises to reach their customers as it is reliable. Bulk SMS Service is cost effective in comparison with any other media as High Return on Investment can be achieved with Low cost. SMS Striker provides both Transactional and Promotional Bulk SMS in adherence with TRAI (Telecom Regulatory Authority of India) as SMS Service Provider. Bulk SMS Services helps in Brand Promotion, for event publicity, to do follow-ups and for Customer Engagement & Retention. SMS Striker also provides API, S.M.P.P and Reseller (White Label Panel Provider) services.

Transactional SMS Service

Generally, Transactional Bulk SMSes are sent to notify or share the information to the customers with regards to the Transactions were made. Transactional Bulk SMS Service is being highly reliable and fastest correspondent, preferred most in OTP services. Transactional SMSes can be sent to DND registered customers also. SMS Striker provides Transactional Bulk SMS services in adherence with TRAI (Telecom Regulatory Authority of India) as SMS Service Provider. Transactional SMSes are sent round the clock through a Sender ID which contains 6-character Alpha-Numeric. As per the analysis of SMS industry, more than 80 % of Bulk SMS businesses happens in Transactional SMS which describes the importance of Transactional Bulk SMS in all the industries.

Promotional SMS Service

Promotional Bulk SMS service is effective in promoting products and services of the businesses and to achieve better return on investments in comparison with other traditional media. Promotional Bulk SMS has an instant and wide reachability and can be sent between 9 am to 9 pm with a Sender ID contains 6 digits. Enterprises can promote offers, discounts and any other related promotional SMSes to the customers. SMS Striker provides Promotional Bulk SMS services in adherence with TRAI (Telecom Regulatory Authority of India) as SMS Service Provider and with the activation of DND (Do Not Disturb) numbers filtration. Promotional Bulk SMS services has unlimited market potential and are cost effective. SMS Striker provides user friendly and simple User Interface that helps clients to send Promotional Bulk SMSes with lot of ease. Added to this, we are also providing Short URL and Missed Call services that add more value to our Client’s businesses.

Reseller Missed Call SMS Service

A virtual number is provided to Reseller Client and when a customer calls, the call gets disconnected after a ring and the customer is not charged. For an acknowledgement of the missed call, the customer is responded by a SMS instantly and the number is also saved in the database for any further communication.

SMS Striker Services are well crafted by keeping in view the reseller’s needs and requirements in mind. By opting for missed call option Reseller client can get customers in a very short period of time and customers can save on their running cost. Furthermore, it is very helpful for the end users who are looking for information at no cost.

Transactional SMS

Transactional SMS will be sent to DND registered numbers also which doesn’t include any promotional content in it. However, if the customers have opt-in with regards to any offers related to any products of their choice, such content can be sent through Transactional SMS Gateway. Such SMSes are vastly used in education, e-commerce sectors and also for booking confirmation etc, that are meant for passing the required information. Banks and other financial institutions can notify their customers about the necessary information for balance enquiry or to upgrade the credit limit etc.

Why Transactional SMS

  • Round the Clock

    The vital aspect SMS is that they are delivered 24/7 instantly.

  • DLR updates

    Instant Delivery updates can be well known once the SMSes are sent.

  • Re-try mechanism

    Re-try mechanism helps to make sure that Transactional messages are delivered on time over multiple network routes that have less traffic ensuring faster and smoother delivery to the recipients.

  • Delivered to DND

    Transactional messages are delivered to DND also as they are of high importance and sent to those who have opt-in.

  • Builds Brand Loyalty

    Businesses can brand well their products and services as Sender Id which consists of the first two letters of the enterprise is included in the SMS.

  • Excel plug-in

    Excel Plug-in makes it easy and simple in sending Bulk Transactional SMS.

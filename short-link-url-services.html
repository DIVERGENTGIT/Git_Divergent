Short URL

Direct your users to a pre-decided destination. The final destination can be a web page, image, video or audio. Enhancing connectivity with crisp descriptions in your short messages.

The beauty of the feature is its efficiency in profiling and collecting user information. As soon as the Short URL gets clicked, you can track the number of clicks and the underlying information like the user’s location, kind of handset and type of Operating System. Such information is useful for businesses to create a more targeted and customized marketing strategies.

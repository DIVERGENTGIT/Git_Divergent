About Us

SMS Striker is a subsidiary of the Telecom service provider Striker Soft Solutions. It was established in 2009 and since then it has pioneered innovative technology-based solutions.

Innovative
Technology-based
Solutions

Striker Soft Solutions Pvt Ltd. is your trusted bridge to your clients, with its end-to-end Enterprise Messaging and Voice Solutions that allow you to reach your Clients and Customers through Mobile Services like Text and Voice Solutions.

It is the Mission of SMS Striker to help Clients across industries reduce the cost of reliable and effective communication with customers.  Over the years the company's competitive zeal and enthusiasm has helped it carve a comfortable niche in the IT industry.

SMS Striker has its offices in 3 locations with Hyderabad being its Headquarter. It has over 3000 international and domestic clients and a growing network of more than 50 vendors across the globe.

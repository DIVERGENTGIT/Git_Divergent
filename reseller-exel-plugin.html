Reseller Bulk SMS Excel Spreadsheet Plug-in

With SMS Striker, your clients can send Bulk SMS Excel spreadsheet which contains the required information in various columns. By using the spreadsheet your client can select excel file of their choice and type messages and send it. SMS Excel Plug-in helps your clients in making their task of sending Bulk SMS easy and simple. SMS Striker Excel Plug-in well integrates with Microsoft Excel and helps your clients to send SMS to their respective target customer’s mobile phones.

This is useful for all of your clients let it be a stock broking firm, a school, a financial institution to send messages that are customized as per their customers. Your clients, with their customer data in MS-Excel can now send SMS from any part of the world and all they need is a computer connected to the internet and SMS-Excel Plug-in which is integrated to the MS-Excel.

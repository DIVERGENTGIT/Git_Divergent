Short URL

The Short URL is a simple and powerful tool that enhances connectivity, lead management and takes the marketing campaign closer to sales and closure. The features and specifications that are contained in Short URL makes it a result oriented. Striker Short URL includes One URL integrating with another URL through mapping which is designed by us.

As soon as a user clicks on the Short URL, it helps in profiling and collecting user information like location of user, kind of handset, OS, etc., being used. Based on these factors, a company may customize its marketing campaign and messaging to make it more meaningful to the consumer. Your ROI (Return on Investment) can be well known with the use of Short URL.

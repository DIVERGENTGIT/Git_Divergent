Promotional SMS are sent with the objective of promoting your products and services and also to send discounts, offers or any other promotions to the customers.This type of messages are delivered to Non-DND numbers. Furthermore it can be sent only between 9 am to 9 pm. Promotional SMS helps in branding as well as to boost your sales and is one of the cost effective marketing solutions.

Example of
Promotional SMS includes

  • Dear <customer name>, Get Flat 20 % off on all our products. Buy now from our store
  • Dear <customer name>, We have sent you a discount coupon to your email address. Hurry before the discount coupon expires

Important features

  • Promotional SMS is suitable for all kinds and sizes of business like start-up, small scale, mid-sized or large scale enterprises.
  • You can directly send your promotional sms to your target customers
  • To send promotional SMS, all it requires a Internet connectivity and within no time the messages are delivered to the users.
  • You can spread your product, brand in a single click and within a second.
  • The promotional messages are useful in sending seasons discounts and offers.

Discover SMS with missed call which is proven to be one of the effective strategy in promoting your products and services. As per this, Missed call Number is a Voice based Virtual Number and it can be used in the form of mobile or a landline number. The term Virtual is used here in this context because this type of number can be operated remotely i.e from any location and possess the feature of redirecting calls instantly. Reflecting your business in a more professional appearance is possible with Virtual Number.

With the use of Virtual Phone number there is a sense of local presence in regard to your business among your customers. Moreover Virtual number helps in managing all your conversations from a single number and there is no need of new hardware. When you run bulk sms campaigns, there is always a possibility of receiving high calls and in this process the chances of missing calls are too high. To avoid this type of instance, Virtual number is used wherein each and every call can be captured and saved in the database for future reference. Furthermore, if you receive any number of calls in a single second, 24/7 round the clock also each and every call is well captured and at any point of time there is no possibility of missing in capturing the calls.

As missed call numbers are stored, you can call back to your customers at their comfortable time. Though you have smart phones with many advanced features, this type of feature that includes capturing number of calls at once is only available in Virtual Numbers.

Added to this, all missed calls can be acknowledged by sending required details through sms to the respective customers who have given it and which serves the purpose of the customer. Example includes balance enquiry and app downloading.

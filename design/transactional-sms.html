Transactional SMS refers to messages that are sent to the users who have opt-in for the services as an acknowledgement in regard to the actions performed by them. One can send generic, informative and any other important information through transactional messages.

You can send alerts, transactional codes, OTPs only to the users that are registered. We provide Transaction SMS for Business to Business and Business to customers. Our Transactional SMS services are 24/7 and are very trustworthy as we adhere strictly to TRAI Regulations. You are provided a Sender Identification which is a 6-character Alphabetic by us. Furthermore, we provide excellent third party API integration.

Transactional SMS will be sent to DND registered numbers also and it should not include any Promotional SMS Content in it. However, if the customers has opt-in in regard to receive any offers related to any products of their choice such content can be sent through Transactional SMS Gateway. Transactional sms are used for sending in education, e-commerce, booking confirmation etc and are meant for passing the required information and sharing the records to the customers. Banks and other financial institutions can notify their customers about the necessary information and the balance left in their account.

Example of
Transactional SMS includes

  • When any customer placed an order in any e-commerce website, the customer receives Order confirmation message.
  • If the user has signed up with your website - You can send the user one time password (OTP) message.
  • If the users requests you to alert them when any event happens, you can send them sms alerts on the happening of that particular Event.

Important features

  • Delivering any urgent information
  • There is no time constraint while sending transactional SMS
  • With Delivery report you can know how many the Transactional messages are reached to your users.
  • Very useful for sending alerts

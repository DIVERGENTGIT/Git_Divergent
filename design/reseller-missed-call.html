The virtual number is provided to your client and when customer calls, the call gets disconnected after a ring and the customer is not charged. For an acknowledgement of the missed call, the customer can be responded by a sms instantly and the number is also saved in the database for any further communication.

SMS Striker services are well crafted by keeping in view the reseller’s needs and requirements in mind. By opting for missed call option your client can get customers in a very short period of time and customers can save on their running cost. Furthermore, it is very helpful for the end users who are looking for information at no cost.

This type of missed call services are useful for your clients depending on their needs and requirements. For some instances include.

Missed call service is an effective way to involve and engage customers. We offer one of the reliable and rich feature missed call services for our Resellers, which in turn helps our reseller customers.

  • Mobile Number Verification

    It is one of the simple and easy processes for both businesses and for the consumers by avoiding lengthy form fillings and procedure.

  • Lead Generation

    By including your missed call service number in all your advertisements you can well capture all missed calls with the usage of Virtual Number.

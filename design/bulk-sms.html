Bulk SMS

Bulk sms service includes sending a message to multiple mobile's via internet. The usage of SMS in our daily lives is of enormous importance and today it is not only the most vital forms of communication but also the most popular and prevalent among us. Furthermore, it is one of the fastest and most trusted communication tools globally, because of its speed and reachability. SMS are read by 98% of the customers and it is very cost effective when compared to other forms of communication. SMS works across on all operating systems and on all phone types and with SMS campaigns you can discover high response and conversion rate.

Choose our Bulk SMS India, as we provide our Bulk sms services in accordance to the regulations of TRAI (Telecom Regulatory Authority of India). SMS Striker make it incredibly easy, as you can track delivery status for all your SMS campaign and our services enable you to send bulk sms with lot of ease.

Transactional SMS

Transactional SMS refers to messages that are sent to the users who have opt-in for the services as an acknowledgement in regard to the actions performed by them. You can send transactional SMS 24/7. Transactional SMS services that we provide are trustworthy and we adhere strictly to TRAI Regulations.

You are provided a Sender Identification which is of 6 Alphabetic characters by us as it will help your customers to know from which enterprise the sms is sent. You can deliver this Transactional SMS to DND numbers also.

Promotional SMS

Now you can brand your product and services at a short period and promotional sms is one of the best ways to reach your customers directly. All our promotional SMS are provided keeping in strict adherence of TRAI (Telecom Regulatory Authority of India). An ideal promotional SMS includes a sender Id with an 6 numeric characters.

Promotional sms services are one of the effective ways to promote your products and services. It is one of the cost-effective and reliable marketing tools.This type of messages includes sending any message for promoting, advertising, upselling your products and services. Promotional messages are not delivered to the users who have registered TRAI DND and are delivered only between 9 am to 9 pm.
